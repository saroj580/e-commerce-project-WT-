Our Story

Founded in 2025, Lume started with a simple mission: to make quality fashion accessible to everyone. What began as a small startup in Kathmandu has grown into a thriving online marketplace serving customers across Nepal.

We believe that looking good shouldn't cost a fortune. Our team works tirelessly to source the best products at competitive prices, ensuring that our customers always get exceptional value without compromising on quality.

As we've grown, our commitment to customer satisfaction has remained unwavering. Every decision we make is guided by our desire to create the best possible shopping experience for you.

2025

Lume was founded with just 4 team members and 50 products and within a 2 months we have reached 50,000 customers and expanded nationwide

Our Mission

To provide high-quality fashion at affordable prices, making style accessible to everyone regardless of budget. We aim to simplify the shopping experience through innovative technology and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's leading online fashion destination, known for our quality products, ethical practices, and customer-first approach. We envision a future where shopping is effortless, enjoyable, and sustainable.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We are honest and transparent in all our dealings
  • Quality: We never compromise on the standard of our products
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve
  • Sustainability: We are committed to ethical and eco-friendly practices
